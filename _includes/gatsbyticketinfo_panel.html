THE GREAT GATSBY EXPERIENCE is an hour-long, immersive theatrical production that blends live theatre and audience interaction, whisking you away right into the heart of Jay Gatsby's legendary parties. Whether you're ready to shine in the spotlight or prefer to soak in the spectacle, the choice is entirely yours! Engage with the characters or simply marvel as the story unfolds around you. Either way... you're in for an unforgettable night!

The experience will be on your feet, exploring the outside property freely with scenes and actors all around. There are no stairs, but the terrain is varied and includes a brick path, a gravel driveway, and some patches of grass. Please dress accordingly.


It will be a one hour long event. The performance starts on the front porch with an opening scene and then spreads across the property from there, with more scenes and audience interactions all around, before returning to the front porch again for an epic conclusion!


Family friendly immersive experience with music.
