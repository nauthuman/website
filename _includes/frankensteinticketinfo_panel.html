THE FRANKENSTEIN EXPERIENCE is an hour-long, immersive theatrical production that blends live theatre and audience interaction. You’ll walk the grounds where science and madness blur, and witness the spark of life – and horror – ignite before your eyes. Test scientific theories with the Doctor and his demented Assistant, learn to love with the Creature and his Bride, and discover dark conspiracies with the Doctor's discarded failed Creations. Whether you choose to interact or simply watch the story unfold around you, every corner holds a secret waiting to be discovered. Dare to enter the laboratory… and you may never leave again.

The experience will be on your feet, exploring the outside property freely with scenes and actors all around. The terrain is varied and includes a stairs, a brick path, a gravel driveway, and some patches of grass. Please dress accordingly.


The performance starts on the front porch with an opening scene and then spreads across the property from there, with more scenes and audience interactions all around, before returning to the front porch again for an epic conclusion!
