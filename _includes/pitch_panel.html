Naut Human Productions is an Atlanta-based production company that specializes in dark content of all kinds: feature films, television, music videos, commercials, branded content, etc. If you can dream it, we can bring it to life!

We aim to serve high-quality and micro-budget content to anyone in Atlanta with a creative goal, while providing work and opportunities for both early-career Georgia filmmakers and local film students from the University of Georgia and the Georgia Film Academy.
